About Willow Creek

Nestled among whispering pines and winding trails, Willow Creek Bed & Breakfast offers the charm of a rustic mountain escape with the comfort of thoughtfully appointed rooms. Our mornings begin with a homemade breakfast featuring local ingredients, freshly brewed coffee, and the scent of warm pastries drifting through the lodge.

Spend your days exploring nearby hiking paths and scenic overlooks, then return to crackling firelight under a star-filled sky. Whether you are planning a romantic weekend or a quiet family retreat, our cozy spaces and warm hospitality make it easy to unwind and reconnect.
